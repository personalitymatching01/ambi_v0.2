Live — Research & Feasibility Phase

Dating has become an exercise in consumption. Swipe through hundreds of faces. Exchange the same hollow openers. Build a version of yourself optimised for a thumbnail. Pay to be seen by people you haven't met yet. It keeps people busy. It rarely gets them anywhere real.

We think genuine compatibility — the kind that leads to actual intimacy, actual conversation, actual relationships — deserves more than an algorithm tuned for engagement. It deserves one built for fit.

That's what Zaphyr is building. For now, there is no app — this is a proof of concept. We are collecting personality profiles to validate the model. Once the pool is ready, we will identify who belongs together and reach out directly by WhatsApp or email, putting people in touch and arranging a first date at a real venue. No platform to download. No ghosting. No performance.

The questionnaire

Meet AMBI.

The AMBI is a multi-dimensional personality inventory designed to go beneath the surface. Most personality tests tell you what type you are. AMBI maps how you work — alone, with others, and in the life you've built — so we can find someone who genuinely complements you.

Scientific basis Yarkoni, T. (2010). The Abbreviation of Personality, or how to Measure 200 Personality Scales with 200 Items. Journal of Research in Personality, 44(2), 180–198. doi.org/10.1016/j.jrp.2010.01.002
🧠
Who you are as an individual
Your core personality structure — how you process the world, where you draw energy from, how you make decisions, how you handle complexity and uncertainty.
🤝
Who you are in relationships
Your relational patterns — how you attach, how you communicate when things are hard, what you need to feel safe, and how you show care.
🌿
What you love and how you live
Your interests, values, and the texture of your daily life — because compatibility isn't only about psychology. It's also about actually enjoying each other's company.
